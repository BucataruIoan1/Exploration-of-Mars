Story

In the year 2200, with Earth facing dire consequences from climate change and overpopulation, humans turn to Mars as a potential refuge. After a decade of preparation, a large-scale mission to colonize Mars begins, with 30 rockets carrying astronauts and materials. However, only 10 rockets successfully reach Mars due to numerous challenges along the journey.

Despite the establishment of the first Earth colony on Mars, the harsh environment takes its toll. Extraterrestrial attacks, radiation, and sandstorms damage the colony's structures, requiring materials from Earth for repairs. As Earth itself plunges into chaos with nuclear war between major powers, the colony's survival becomes humanity's last hope. Repeated attempts to send aid fail due to the ongoing war.

Finally, on June 21, 2213, the USA launches the StarLight rocket to rescue the colony. However, the rocket crashes on Mars during an asteroid storm, leaving only one survivor, a doctor, and an injured engineer. The team must now navigate the treacherous Martian terrain to deliver crucial materials to the colony.

How to Play

In this game, players navigate characters across Mars, restricted to moving one square at a time in four directions: UP, DOWN, LEFT, or RIGHT. Each character selects one action from EXPLORE, STAY, HEAL, or REPAIR per turn.

The Explorer specializes in exploration only, ideal for scouting the Martian landscape. The Doctor can heal, explore cautiously, and remain stationary, but must beware of their damaged spacesuit. The Engineer possesses skills in exploration, repairs, and remaining stationary, but must also exercise caution due to injuries. Hazards on Mars, such as sandstorms, radiation, extraterrestrial attacks, slipping off rocks, and encounters with Darth Vader, pose threats to the characters. It's essential to manage life and spacesuit functionality, seeking healing from the Doctor when life is low and repairs from the Engineer when spacesuit functionality deteriorates.
