How to Play

🎯 Objective

Your team of 3 heroes must explore a 5×5 Mars grid to find the hidden colony. Move one hero per turn. Win by stepping on the colony cell. Lose if all 3 heroes reach 0 HP.

🔄 Turn Order

Explorer → Doctor → Engineer (repeat). Dead heroes are skipped. Each turn you pick one action.

👥 Your Heroes

👨‍🚀

Explorer

Scout-only. Can only Explore. No healing or staying.

HP 100 | Armour 100

🧑‍⚕️

Doctor

Can Explore, Stay, or Heal a teammate on the same cell (+50 HP).

HP 100 | Armour 25

👨‍🔧

Engineer

Can Explore, Stay, or Repair a teammate on the same cell (+50 Armour).

HP 25 | Armour 100

⚠️ Dangers

Each unexplored cell may hide a trap. Damage triggers once (first visit).

🛡️ Armour Decay

If a hero has 0 Armour, they lose 10 HP every 5 seconds (won't drop below 10 HP from this alone).
